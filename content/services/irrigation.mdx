## Which System is For You?

The right irrigation system for you depends on what you’re aiming to deliver hydration to. High and low flow are the two main categories for sprinkler systems, and depending on your garden you can use one of these types, or a combination of both.

For example, if your plants only require a small amount of water over a long period of time, A Cut Above Lawn Care Inc might recommend a drip system, which dispenses only a minimal amount of water, and can either be controlled manually or set up to an automatic system.

For thirstier plants, overhead sprinklers could be the right option. The major benefit of these systems comes from their even distribution of water. If you’ve ever noticed patches of grass that are brown and dry, while other areas are green and healthy, an overhead sprinkler could be the solution to your problems. Because they deliver a uniform amount of water to everything in their reach, no area of your lawn will receive more or less water than another area.
