# Your Lawn Care Professionals

A luscious, thick lawn is the hallmark of a well-run property, but establishing healthy and consistent growth involves time and dedication. Condo corporations, property management companies, and commercial clients understand this — and rarely have the bandwidth to handle it themselves. That’s why we started A Cut Above Lawn Care Inc over 27 years ago. Our goal is to help our clients attain the grounds they deserve through high quality work and a communicative approach to every project.

## Landscaping and Yard Services

Nobody should need to juggle multiple contractors for a single property. A Cut Above Lawn Care Inc handles it all under one roof:

- **Hardscape;** patios and walkways are just the beginning of what our team is capable of.
- **Irrigation;** maintaining the health of your lawn is great but overwatering can actually harm the appearance of your yard and even cause damage to your property.
- **Landscaping;** from planting new shrubberies to garden installations, our landscaping services can meet the most particular needs.
- **Lawn care;** cutting the lawn is something few of us have time for. At A Cut Above Lawn Care Inc our expert team provides regular scheduled (or one time) cutting, weeding, and upkeep.
